Client: Quick question about pricing: if I have one session for a price, and then sell a 4-session bundle, what’s the incentive for a client to buy the full bundle instead of one at a time?

Christo: Pricing deals depend on industry margins, but as a service-based business you have more flexibility. Generally, if you sell things one at a time, a lot of people will just stop after one or two, especially if it’s not packaged. So, push the package confidently—if four sessions gets better results, position the package as your main offer. If you go too extreme with the discount, it looks suspicious; too little, not enough incentive.
