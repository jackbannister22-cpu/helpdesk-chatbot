Christo: If you’re asking for a platform review like Google or TripAdvisor, give them an angle—a specific prompt helps massively. If you’re looking to gather your own testimonials (for your site or collateral), I recommend four key questions:

2. Why did you choose us?
3. What did you love the most or what benefits did you get?
