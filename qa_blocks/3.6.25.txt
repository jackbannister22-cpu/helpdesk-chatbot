Question:
What’s the purpose and best practice for the Postcard Challenge in client relationship management?
Answer:
The Postcard Challenge involves sending 40 handwritten cards in 40 days to clients, suppliers, or potential partners. The point is to nurture relationships, express genuine appreciation, and stand out—without any sales pitch. For clients, focus on warmth and thoughtfulness; for partners, personalize it by referencing something about them. Use the “fridge test”: Would the card be worth displaying?
Key Insight:
Handwritten, personal outreach taps into reciprocity and creates memorable, genuine connections in a digital world.
