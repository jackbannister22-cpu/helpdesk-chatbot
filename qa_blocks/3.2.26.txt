Question: How can Sharon increase repeat business and justify her high hourly rates for coaching and tutoring?
Answer: Sharon can shift from purely hourly, in-person lessons to a hybrid model: providing some face-to-face sessions while delivering additional value through online resources, courses, and trainings that don't require her direct time for every session. This allows her to charge more and reduce face-to-face hours. Structuring offerings into programs (beginner, intermediate, advanced), creating video content, and using those videos both as course material and for marketing/social media all help. Clarifying the value proposition and using testimonials can further justify higher pricing and encourage client retention.
Key Insight: By packaging her services and leveraging online resources, Sharon can scale her business, maintain high price points, and increase recurring revenue without being as tied to in-person, hourly work.
